# Tools & Services Catalog

## Platform-Native Tools

### Google

| Tool | Purpose | Access |
|------|---------|--------|
| Google Takeout | Export all Google data before deletion | takeout.google.com |
| Results about you | Monitor and remove personal info from search results | Google Search settings |
| Account deletion | Full account removal | myaccount.google.com |
| Outdated content removal | Refresh search results after source changes | Google Search Console |

### Microsoft/Bing

| Tool | Purpose | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Bing content removal | Request search result removal | Limited — recommends publisher removal first |

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)

| Tool | Purpose | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Account deletion | Remove Facebook account and data | Distinct from deactivation |
| Instagram deletion | Remove Instagram account | Linked to Meta infrastructure |
| Download Your Information | Export data before deletion | Available in settings |

### X (Twitter)

| Tool | Purpose | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Account deactivation | 30-day window before permanent deletion | Recoverable during window |

## Regulatory/Government Tools

| Tool | Jurisdiction | Purpose | Status |
|------|-------------|---------|--------|
| California DROP | California, USA | Bulk deletion from registered data brokers | Live; mandatory broker compliance Aug 1, 2026 |
| Global Privacy Control (GPC) | California + expanding | Browser-based opt-out signal | Supported in Firefox natively |

## Paid Automation Services

| Service | Model | What It Does |
|---------|-------|-------------|

| Incogni | Subscription | Automates broker removal requests; continuous monitoring |
